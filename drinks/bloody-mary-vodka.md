# Sous Vide Bloody Mary Vodka

[!badge text="Makes ~750 mL" variant="info"] [!badge text="Make Ahead" variant="warning"] [!badge text="~3 hr active" variant="success"]

Get all the flavors of a great Bloody Mary without mixers — infused right into the vodka. Pro tip: use high quality vodka.

*By Emily Farris and Jeff Akin, co-founders of Feed Me Creative.*

---

## Equipment

- Sous vide precision cooker (Anova or similar)
- Large zip-top bag or vacuum sealer
- Fine-mesh strainer
- Airtight storage bottle

---

## Ingredients

- 1 (750 mL) bottle vodka
- 6 Roma tomatoes, quartered
- 1 anaheim pepper, stemmed, seeds removed, sliced into ½-inch pieces
- ¼ onion, peeled and sliced into ½-inch pieces
- 6 whole garlic cloves, peeled
- 1 jalapeño pepper, sliced in half, seeds removed
- 1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
- Zest of 3 large limes

---

## Instructions

1. **Set the Anova** Sous Vide Precision Cooker to **145°F (62.8°C)**.
2. **Bag it** — combine all ingredients in a large zip-top bag. Seal the bag using the water immersion technique.
3. **Sous vide** for **3 hours**.
4. **Strain** — when the timer goes off, remove the bag from the water bath. Strain the contents through a fine-mesh strainer into a large bowl. Let cool to room temperature.
5. **Bottle** — transfer to an airtight storage bottle and refrigerate overnight before serving.

!!!tip Keeps for months
Store in an airtight bottle in the refrigerator for up to six months. Shake or stir before using.
!!!

!!!info Serving suggestion
Use in place of plain vodka in any Bloody Mary recipe. The tomato, pepper, and lime are already in the spirit — just add Bloody Mary mix or tomato juice and garnish as usual.
!!!
