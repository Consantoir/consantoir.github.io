# Christmas Dinner

[!badge text="Dairy-Free" variant="success"] [!badge text="Gluten-Free" variant="info"] [!badge text="Serves 6–8" variant="secondary"]

A full Christmas feast — herb-seared beef tenderloin, all the sides, and two dessert options.

---

==- 🥩 Herb-Garlic Beef Tenderloin

[!badge text="Main Course" variant="contrast"]

+++ Ingredients
- 3.5–4 lb beef tenderloin
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp kosher salt
- 2 tsp cracked black pepper
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 tsp dried rosemary
- ½ tsp mustard powder
- ½ tsp porcini mushroom powder *(optional — adds deep umami)*
+++ Instructions
1. Mix all dry rub ingredients together in a bowl.
2. Pat tenderloin dry and trim any silver skin.
3. Rub with olive oil, then coat evenly with the rub.
4. Refrigerate uncovered overnight.
5. Remove from fridge 60–90 minutes before cooking.
6. Sear on all sides in a hot cast iron pan.
7. Roast at 425°F for 20–28 minutes until internal temp reaches **125–130°F**.
8. Rest 15 minutes, then slice and serve.
+++

==- 🫙 Dairy-Free Horseradish Cream

[!badge text="Sauce" variant="ghost"]

+++ Ingredients
- 1 cup coconut cream
- ½ cup DF cream cheese or cashew cream
- 2–3 tbsp prepared horseradish
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt & pepper to taste
+++ Instructions
1. Whip coconut cream and cream cheese together until smooth.
2. Fold in horseradish, Dijon, lemon juice, and seasoning.
3. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.
+++

!!!tip Make it ahead
This sauce can be made up to 2 days in advance — it actually gets better as the flavors meld.
!!!

==- 🥔 Instant Pot Garlic Mashed Potatoes

[!badge text="Side" variant="ghost"]

+++ Ingredients
- 5 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes
- 1½ cups water or broth
- 6 tbsp dairy-free butter
- 1–1¼ cups dairy-free milk
- 3 tbsp roasted garlic
- 1½ tsp salt
+++ Instructions
1. Add potatoes, water, and salt to the Instant Pot.
2. Pressure cook on HIGH for 12 minutes; quick release.
3. Drain, then mash with DF butter, milk, and roasted garlic.
4. Adjust seasoning and serve hot.
+++

==- 🍮 GF + DF Yorkshire Puddings

[!badge text="Side" variant="ghost"] [!badge text="GF" variant="info"] [!badge text="DF" variant="success"]

+++ Ingredients
- 1 cup GF 1:1 flour
- 1 cup dairy-free milk
- 4 eggs
- ½ tsp salt
- 4 tbsp beef drippings
+++ Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Add beef drippings to a muffin tin and heat in oven until smoking hot.
3. Whisk flour, milk, eggs, and salt into a smooth batter.
4. Carefully pour batter into the hot tins, filling halfway.
5. Bake 20–25 minutes. Do not open the oven.
+++

!!!warning Don't open the oven!
Opening the oven during baking will cause the Yorkshire puddings to deflate. Set a timer and resist.
!!!

==- 🥕 Maple-Thyme Carrots

[!badge text="Side" variant="ghost"]

+++ Ingredients
- 2 lbs carrots, peeled and cut on the diagonal
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp fresh thyme
- Salt & pepper to taste
+++ Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Toss carrots with olive oil, maple syrup, thyme, salt, and pepper.
3. Spread on a baking sheet and roast 25–30 minutes until caramelized.
+++

==- 🌿 Pomegranate Balsamic Brussels Sprouts

[!badge text="Side" variant="ghost"]

+++ Ingredients
**Sprouts**
- 2 lbs Brussels sprouts, halved
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- 6–7 slices bacon *(optional)*

**Glaze**
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- ¼ cup pomegranate juice
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp rosemary
+++ Instructions
1. Toss sprouts with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Add bacon if using.
2. Roast at 425°F for 25–30 minutes.
3. While roasting, reduce balsamic, pomegranate juice, maple syrup, and rosemary in a saucepan until syrupy.
4. Toss roasted sprouts in glaze and return to oven 3–4 minutes.
+++

==- 🍎 Spiced Apple Crumble

[!badge text="Dessert" variant="danger"] [!badge text="GF" variant="info"] [!badge text="DF" variant="success"]

+++ Ingredients
**Filling**
- 6–7 apples, peeled and sliced
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 1½ tsp cinnamon
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

**Crumble Topping**
- 1 cup GF oats
- ½ cup almond flour
- ⅓ cup brown sugar
- 5 tbsp dairy-free butter
+++ Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Toss apple slices with maple syrup, cinnamon, and lemon juice. Spread in a baking dish.
3. Mix oats, almond flour, brown sugar, and DF butter until crumbly. Spread over apples.
4. Bake 35–40 minutes until golden and bubbling.
5. Serve warm with dairy-free ice cream or coconut whipped cream.
+++

==- 🍷 Poached Pears in Spiced Merlot

[!badge text="Dessert" variant="danger"] [!badge text="GF" variant="info"] [!badge text="DF" variant="success"] [!badge text="Elegant" variant="ghost"]

+++ Ingredients
- 6 pears, peeled with stems intact
- 1 bottle Merlot
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 2 star anise
- 4 cloves
- Orange peel from 1 orange
+++ Instructions
1. Combine Merlot, sugar, cinnamon, star anise, cloves, and orange peel in a deep saucepan.
2. Bring to a simmer and stir until sugar dissolves.
3. Add pears and poach 20–25 minutes, turning occasionally, until tender.
4. Remove pears and reduce the poaching liquid to a syrup.
5. Serve pears drizzled with the warm syrup.
+++

!!!tip Make it ahead
Pears can be poached 1–2 days ahead and stored in the syrup in the fridge. Gently reheat before serving.
!!!
