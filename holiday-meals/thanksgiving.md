# Thanksgiving

A full Thanksgiving spread. All recipes are dairy-free, gluten-free, and soy-free. Includes a day-of cooking timeline for a 4:00 PM dinner.

## Recipes

==- 🍖 Maple-Dijon Spiral Ham

+++ Ham Ingredients
- 1 spiral-sliced ham *(8–10 lb)*
- ½ cup apple juice, apple cider, or water
- Foil for covering and lining the pan
+++ Glaze Ingredients
- ½ cup maple syrup
- ½ cup brown sugar or coconut sugar
- ¼ cup Dijon or whole-grain mustard *(GF)*
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- ½ tsp ground cloves *(optional)*
- ½ tsp cinnamon
+++ Instructions
**Make the glaze first:**
1. Combine all glaze ingredients in a small saucepan.
2. Simmer over medium heat ~5 minutes, stirring until slightly thickened. Set aside.

**Cook the ham:**
1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Line roasting pan with foil and place rack inside.
2. Pour ½ cup apple juice or cider into the bottom of the pan.
3. Place ham cut-side down on the rack and cover loosely with foil.
4. Bake for 10–12 minutes per pound.
5. Remove foil for the last 30 minutes and brush glaze every 10 minutes.
6. Tent with foil and rest 10–15 minutes before slicing.
+++

==- 🥔 Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes

+++ Ingredients
- 3 lb Yukon Gold or Russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 1 cup water or broth
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ cup oat milk or coconut milk
- ¼ cup olive oil or dairy-free butter
- Salt & pepper to taste
+++ Instructions
1. Add potatoes, water, and salt to the Instant Pot.
2. Seal lid and pressure cook on HIGH for 10 minutes.
3. Quick release pressure and drain potatoes.
4. Return to pot; let steam dissipate 1–2 minutes.
5. Mash with dairy-free butter and milk until smooth.
6. Season to taste and keep warm.
+++

==- 🫙 Gluten-Free Gravy

+++ Ingredients
- 2 tbsp olive oil or dairy-free butter
- 2 tbsp gluten-free flour
- 2 cups broth *(vegetable, chicken, or turkey)*
- 1 tbsp coconut aminos
- Salt & pepper to taste
+++ Instructions
1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Whisk in GF flour to form a roux; cook 1 minute.
3. Gradually whisk in broth until smooth.
4. Add coconut aminos, salt, and pepper.
5. Simmer 3–5 minutes until thickened.
+++

==- 🍠 Mashed Yams with Marshmallows

+++ Ingredients
- 3 lb yams or sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
- ¼ cup maple syrup or brown sugar
- ¼ cup coconut cream or oat milk
- 3 tbsp olive oil or dairy-free butter
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- ¼ tsp nutmeg
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1½–2 cups vegan marshmallows
- ½ cup chopped pecans or pumpkin seeds *(optional)*
+++ Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Boil yams 15–20 minutes until tender; drain.
3. Mash with sweetener, coconut cream, DF butter, vanilla, spices, and salt.
4. Spread into a baking dish and top with marshmallows *(and nuts if using)*.
5. Bake 15–20 minutes until marshmallows are golden.
+++

==- 🥦 Balsamic Roasted Brussels Sprouts

+++ Ingredients
- 2 lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
+++ Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Toss sprouts in olive oil, salt, and pepper.
3. Roast 25 minutes, stirring halfway.
4. Simmer balsamic vinegar and maple syrup 5 minutes to reduce.
5. Drizzle glaze over sprouts before serving.
+++

==- 🥕 Herbed Roasted Carrots

+++ Ingredients
- 2 lb carrots, peeled and cut
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1 tsp rosemary
- Salt & pepper
+++ Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Toss carrots with olive oil, herbs, salt, and pepper.
3. Roast 25–30 minutes, stirring halfway.
4. Serve warm.
+++
