# Crispy Parmesan–Panko Air Fryer Pork Chops

[!badge text="Serves 5" variant="info"] [!badge text="Air Fryer" variant="warning"] [!badge text="~15 min" variant="success"]

Boneless, ¼-inch thick pork chops with a crispy golden parmesan-panko crust. Fast, foolproof, and packed with flavor.

---

## Ingredients

+++ Binder
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
+++ Breading
- 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
- ½ cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- ¾ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp onion powder
- ½ tsp paprika *(smoked preferred)*
- ½ tsp black pepper
- ½ tsp Italian seasoning *(optional)*
+++ Protein & Oil
- 5 boneless pork chops *(¼ inch thick)*
- Extra-virgin olive oil *(pump sprayer)*
+++

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat** air fryer to 400°F (205°C) for 3–5 minutes.
2. **Dry** the pork chops completely with paper towels.
3. **Make binder** — mix mayonnaise and Dijon mustard until smooth.
4. **Make breading** — combine all breading ingredients in a shallow dish and mix well.
5. **Coat** each chop with the mayo–Dijon binder, then press firmly into breading on both sides.
6. **Spray** the air fryer basket lightly with olive oil. Arrange chops in a single layer and mist the tops.
7. **Air fry** 4 minutes. Flip carefully, mist again, and cook 3–4 more minutes until golden brown.
8. **Check temp** — pork is done at **145°F** internal temperature. Rest 2 minutes before serving.

---

!!!tip Tips & Tricks
- **Total cook time:** 7–8 minutes
- Use a **light mist** of oil only — oversaturating causes steaming instead of crisping
- If oil smokes or browns too fast, reduce temperature to **390°F**
!!!
