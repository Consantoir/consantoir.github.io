# Lemon Ricotta Pasta

[!badge text="Serves 6" variant="info"] [!badge text="Vegetarian" variant="success"] [!badge text="30 min" variant="warning"]

Pasta made with spinach, garlic, shallot, lemon, and a simple creamy ricotta sauce. Serve with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil for the perfect pasta dinner.

*From Two Peas and Their Pod.*

---

## Ingredients

+++ Pasta & Base
- 12 oz pasta *(rigatoni, penne, or your favorite shape)*
- ½ cup reserved pasta water
- 1 tbsp tablespoon olive oil
- 3 cups packed spinach leaves
- 1 shallot, minced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
+++ Ricotta Sauce
- 1 cup ricotta cheese
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- ½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
+++ Garnish
- Crushed red pepper flakes *(optional)*
- Extra Parmesan cheese
- Extra squeeze of lemon juice
+++

---

## Instructions

1. **Cook the pasta** — bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta al dente according to package instructions. Before draining, reserve ½ cup of pasta water. Drain and set aside.
2. **Wilt the spinach** — while the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil over medium heat in a large skillet. Add the shallot and cook 2 minutes. Add the spinach and cook until wilted, about 2–3 minutes. Add the garlic and cook 1 minute more.
3. **Make the sauce** — reduce heat to low. Add the ricotta cheese, lemon zest, lemon juice, and Parmesan to the skillet. Add the reserved pasta water and stir until creamy and smooth.
4. **Toss** — add the drained pasta to the skillet and stir until well coated in the ricotta sauce. If the sauce seems thick, add a splash more pasta water.
5. **Taste and adjust** — season with salt and black pepper. Add more lemon juice if you want a stronger lemon flavor.
6. **Serve immediately** — top with crushed red pepper flakes, extra Parmesan, and a squeeze of lemon if desired.

---

!!!tip Pasta water is magic
Don't skip reserving the pasta water — the starch in it is what makes the sauce silky and helps it cling to every noodle.
!!!

!!!info Protein add-ins
Add grilled chicken, shrimp, or white beans to make this a more substantial meal. Stir in at the same time as the pasta.
!!!
