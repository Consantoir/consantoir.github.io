# Sous Vide Tri-Tip with Horseradish Cream Sauce

[!badge text="Serves 4–6" variant="info"] [!badge text="Sous Vide" variant="warning"] [!badge text="Complete Meal" variant="success"]

Vacuum-sealed tri-tip cooked low and slow at 131°F, finished with a hard sear, and served alongside roasted garlic parmesan potatoes and balsamic-glazed Brussels sprouts. A complete dinner that rewards patience.

!!!warning Plan ahead
The tri-tip needs **20–24 hours** in the water bath. Start it the day before — you'll sear and rest it just before serving.
!!!

---

==- 🥩 Sous Vide Tri-Tip — Start 4+ Hours Ahead

+++ Ingredients
**Steak**
- 2–3 lb tri-tip steak *(see note on cook time)*
- 2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 sprigs fresh rosemary *(optional)*
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 2 tbsp olive oil or unsalted butter *(for searing)*

**Horseradish Cream Sauce**
- ½ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup prepared horseradish *(adjust to taste)*
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives *(optional)*
+++ Instructions
1. **Set the water bath** to **131°F (55°C)**.
2. **Season the steak** — rub all over with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and smoked paprika.
3. **Bag it** — vacuum-seal the steak with rosemary sprigs and crushed garlic. Alternatively, use the water displacement method with a zip-top bag.
4. **Sous vide** for **20–24 hours**.
5. **Make the sauce** — while the steak cooks, mix all horseradish cream sauce ingredients in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper, taste and adjust horseradish. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
6. **Sear** — remove steak from bag and pat completely dry. Heat a cast iron skillet over high heat until smoking hot. Add butter or oil and sear 1–2 minutes per side until deeply browned.
7. **Rest** — transfer to a cutting board and rest 5–10 minutes.
8. **Slice** — cut against the grain and serve with horseradish cream sauce.
+++

==- 🥔 Roasted Garlic Parmesan Potatoes

+++ Ingredients
- 1½ lbs baby potatoes, halved
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish
+++ Instructions
1. **Preheat** oven to **425°F (220°C)**.
2. **Season** — toss potatoes with olive oil, garlic, Parmesan, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
3. **Roast** — spread in a single layer on a baking sheet. Roast for 25–30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crispy.
4. **Garnish and serve** — sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve hot.
+++

==- 🥦 Balsamic-Glazed Brussels Sprouts

+++ Ingredients
- 1 lb Brussels sprouts, halved
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1 tbsp honey
- ¼ cup toasted walnuts or pecans *(optional)*
- 2 tbsp dried cranberries *(optional)*
+++ Instructions
1. **Preheat** oven to **400°F (200°C)**.
2. **Season** — toss Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet.
3. **Roast** — for 20–25 minutes, stirring halfway through, until tender and golden brown.
4. **Prepare the glaze** — in a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar and honey.
5. **Glaze** — drizzle glaze over the Brussels sprouts and toss to coat.
6. **Optional garnishes** — top with toasted walnuts or pecans and dried cranberries before serving.
+++

## Meal Preparation Timeline

| When | What |
|---|---|
| Day before (20–24 hrs out) | Season and start sous vide tri-tip |
| 1 hour before serving | Make horseradish cream sauce; refrigerate |
| 40 min before serving | Preheat oven; prep potatoes and Brussels sprouts |
| 35 min before serving | Roast potatoes |
| 25 min before serving | Roast Brussels sprouts |
| 15 min before serving | Glaze Brussels sprouts |
| Just before serving | Pull tri-tip from bath, pat dry, sear, rest, slice |

!!!success The payoff
The sous vide guarantees edge-to-edge perfect doneness every time. The sear at the end adds the crust. You can't overcook it.
!!!
