# Thai-Inspired Noodle Salad

[!badge text="Serves 8" variant="info"] [!badge text="Vegan" variant="success"] [!badge text="Make Ahead" variant="warning"]

Cold soba noodles in a punchy sesame-lime dressing with carrots and peanuts.
Gets better the longer it sits.

---

## Ingredients

+++ Noodles
- 15 oz dried soba noodles
+++ Dressing
- 1¾ tsp dark sesame oil
- ½ cup rice vinegar
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 1 lime, zested and juiced
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tsp red pepper flakes, or to taste
+++ Mix-ins & Toppings
- 3 cups finely grated carrot
- 1 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- ¼ cup coarsely chopped salted peanuts
+++

---

## Instructions

1. Cook soba noodles per package directions. Drain and rinse thoroughly with cold water. Set aside.
2. Whisk together sesame oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce, lime zest and juice, brown sugar, garlic, and red pepper flakes until the sugar dissolves.
3. Cut noodles into roughly 3-inch lengths and toss into the dressing. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
4. Before serving, toss again. If it seems dry, splash with a little extra soy sauce and rice vinegar. Top with carrots, cilantro, and peanuts.

---

!!!tip Make it Japanese instead
Skip the pepper flakes, garlic, and brown sugar — add ¼ cup toasted sesame seeds instead for a cleaner, more delicate flavor.
!!!

!!!info Noodle swap
Ramen noodles work as a substitute for soba in a pinch.
!!!
